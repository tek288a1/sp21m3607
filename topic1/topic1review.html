Relational and Logical Operations

  • Relational operators:
    • <, <=, >, >=, ==, ~=
    • test the nature of relation between two variables
    • results are either true or false (logical variables)
  • Logical operators:
    • operate on logical variables
    • and (&&), or (||), not (~), exclusive or (xor)
  • Essential ingredient in forming conditional statements
